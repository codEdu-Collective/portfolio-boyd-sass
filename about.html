BIO

Boyd Stevens is a central character from the science fiction horror series From, created by John Griffin. He is portrayed as the stoic and resourceful sheriff of a mysterious small town that traps all who enter, forcing its residents to band together to survive. Boyd is often identified by his unwavering sense of duty, troubled past, and the heavy burden of leadership he carries in the face of supernatural dangers. The character is brought to life by Harold Perrineau, whose performance captures Boyd's resilience and vulnerability, making him one of the show's most compelling figures since its premiere in 2022.
